Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. Who We Are and Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how SeZo Games ("we," "our," or "us"), the publisher of Tower Maze, processes information when you use the game on iOS or Android. Tower Maze is intended for players aged 13 and older.

2. Information We Process

  • On-device game data: progress, settings, lives, unlocked or selected cosmetic items, virtual currency, missions, rewards, and notification preferences.
  • Account and profile data: a Firebase-assigned anonymous user ID, your chosen nickname and avatar, referral code and referral records. Your nickname, avatar, and scores may be visible to other players on leaderboards.
  • Cloud and gameplay data: saved progress, scores, chapter progress, stars, leaderboard rank data, and other information needed for cloud save, account sync, leaderboards, and referral rewards when these features are enabled.
  • Purchase data: product identifiers, purchase status, receipts, and technical transaction or validation information. Payments are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play; we do not receive your payment card or bank account details.
  • Advertising data: ad impressions and interactions, device and advertising identifiers, IP address, and approximate location derived from network information as processed by Google AdMob. On iOS, IDFA is available to AdMob only if you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Analytics and usage data: app launches, sessions, screens viewed, levels or chapters started and completed, progression milestones, virtual-currency events, ad and purchase events, and performance measurements.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs, stack traces, device model, operating-system version, app version, and related technical information used to diagnose reliability and performance issues.
  • Support communications: your email address and any information you choose to include if you contact us for support, privacy questions, or a data request.

We do not request precise GPS location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, health data, or payment-card details.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide, save, restore, and synchronize game features.
  • Operate profiles, leaderboards, referrals, rewards, and virtual economy features.
  • Serve, measure, and protect rewarded, banner, and interstitial advertisements.
  • Process and validate in-app purchases and restore eligible purchases.
  • Understand game usage, balance gameplay, fix errors, prevent fraud or abuse, and improve security and service reliability.
  • Respond to support messages and privacy requests.

4. Services and Disclosures

We use service providers that process information on our behalf or under their own terms:

  • Google AdMob: advertising, consent choices, ad delivery, and ad measurement. See Google Advertising Policies and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase: anonymous authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Firestore cloud storage, cloud save, leaderboards, and referrals. See Firebase Privacy and Security.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play: store distribution, in-app purchase processing, receipt or transaction validation, and purchase restoration. See Apple Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Unity Technologies: the game engine and Unity In-App Purchasing integration. See Unity Privacy Policy.

We do not sell personal information for money. Advertising providers may process or share identifiers and ad activity for personalized advertising where permitted by law and by your consent or device settings. Providers may process information in countries other than your own and apply their own retention and security practices.

5. Advertising, Consent, and Tracking Choices

Tower Maze uses Google's User Messaging Platform where required to request and store advertising consent choices and to provide an in-game privacy-options entry point. Depending on your region and choices, AdMob may serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Declining personalized advertising does not remove ads.

On iOS, cross-app tracking and access to IDFA require your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, AdMob can still request ads without IDFA. You can review the permission later in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. On Android, you can manage or reset the advertising ID and ad-personalization choices in your Google or device privacy settings. Where shown, you can reopen the consent form from Settings > Ad Consent inside the game.

6. Storage, Retention, and Deletion

On-device data remains until you clear the app's data or uninstall the game. When cloud features are enabled, account, profile, save, leaderboard, and referral data may be stored in Google Firebase. We retain cloud data while it is needed to provide these features, protect the service, or meet legal obligations. Purchase and transaction records may be retained longer where required for accounting, fraud prevention, dispute handling, or legal compliance. Google, Apple, and Unity apply their own retention policies to information they process.

To delete local data, uninstall Tower Maze or clear its data in your device settings. To request deletion of cloud or account data, email oguzhansezgin.ie@gmail.com with the subject Data deletion and include your in-game nickname and referral code, if available, so we can locate the correct record. We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days, except for information we must retain by law or for legitimate security, fraud-prevention, or transaction-record purposes.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal information, withdraw consent, or appeal a response to a privacy request. You may exercise applicable rights by contacting us. Withdrawing consent does not affect processing that was lawful before withdrawal. You may also complain to your local data-protection authority.

8. Children's Privacy

Tower Maze is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Children under 13 should not use the game. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information to us, contact us so we can investigate and delete it where required.

9. Local Notifications

If you allow notifications, Tower Maze may schedule reminders for lives, daily activities, rewards, offers, or inactivity. These reminders are scheduled and generated on your device; their content is not sent to our servers. You can disable them in the game's Settings or in your device notification settings.

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect information. No internet transmission or storage system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the game, service providers, or legal requirements change. We will post the revised policy on this page, update the date above, and provide additional notice in the game when appropriate.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions, support, or data requests, contact SeZo Games at oguzhansezgin.ie@gmail.com or use the support contact on the game's store page.
